The Benefits of High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Weight Loss Benefits

There are many benefits that come with HIIT, but some of the most important are the weight loss benefits.

For example:

  • HIIT burns calories and fat in a shorter period of time
  • HIIT boosts metabolism
  • HIIT allows you to burn calories and fat hours after you leave the gym
  • HIIT allows you to lose fat and not muscle

General Health Benefits

While weight loss is an important aspect of HIIT, there are other health benefits that come with HIIT.

HIIT is:

  • Good for heart health
  • An effective way for building endurance
  • An effective energy use

Other Benefits

These are other benefits that people don't know about.

HIIT is:

  • Challenging and interesting, so you never lose interest
  • Possible without equipment
  • Versatile so you can choose your own workouts
